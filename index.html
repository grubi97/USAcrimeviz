U.S.A CRIME STATISTIC

This project was created to show number of murders, robbery and other crimes on a map of the United States. All datasets are from the Federal Bureau of Investigation. Some states may miss some information because they were not tracked by the FBI.



The Crime link shows the number of murders and robbery for each state from 2018. Cliking a state creates a barchart and piechart. School shooting link provides a slider, which shows each year where the shooting took place and number of victims. The Police victims link provides info of victims who died by the hands of Police officers from 2013-2019.
